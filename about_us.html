The Uganda Catholic Education Department in the Uganda Catholic Secretariat, established in 1956 is the technical arm of the Education Commission and is mandated to coordinate activities related to education country wide in liaison with the diocesan education departments. It is further obliged to formulate guidelines for diocesan education departments during the formulation of the diocesan education policies and standards as well as monitoring the implementation of the diocesan policies and services to ensure compliance with standards and regulations set by the Uganda Episcopal Conference. It offers technical support supervision, advice and mentoring of the diocesan education departments and institutions, schools as well as networking and lobbying on behalf of the Uganda Episcopal Conference with government, Private for Profit institutions and international agencies to foster development in the education sector. At the national level, the Education Department is responsible for implementing the decisions of the Education Commission and of the General Assembly, implementing policies related to education in response to the vision and mission of the department aligned with that of the Uganda Catholic Secretariat and Uganda Episcopal Conference. Broad Objectives: To promote the functioning of the Education Department of the Uganda Catholic Secretariat To create an environment for disseminating the Reflections and guidelines To promote the dynamic management and sustainability of the Roman Catholic Church Education Services and Institutions ! Vision: “A community of persons who are fully aware of their infinite dignity as children of God and of their responsibility to live and act according to this greatness” Mission: “To promote the integral education of the human person as an experience of knowledge, growth and understanding reality, whose ultimate and total meaning is Jesus Christ”.
